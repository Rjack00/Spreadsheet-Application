Functional Spreadsheet

Functions:

  • Basic operations: +, -, *, /
  • SUM (returns sum of all selected cells)
  • AVERAGE (returns average of selected cells)
  • MEDIAN (returns median of selected cells)
  • EVEN (returns even numbers of selected cells)
  • SOMEEVEN (returns true/false if even numbers exist in selected cells)
  • EVERYEVEN (returns true/false if every number in selected cells is even)
  • FIRSTTWO (returns first two numbers of selected cells)
  • LASTTWO (returns last two numbers of selected cells)
  • HAS2 (returns true/false if the number 2 exists in selected cells)
  • INCREMENT (returns array of selected cell numbers incremented by one)
  • RANDOM (returns a random number between first two numbers of selected cells)
  • RANGE (returns array of range of numbers between first two numbers of selected cells)
  • NODUPES (returns array of selected cells excluding any duplicates)
